Health Insurance Survey Data

This survey was conducted by the Graduate Student Assembly around the end of the spring 2020 semester. Over 700 responses were compiled into the data shown, and we are now using the survey results to talk with key administration about making changes to how health insurance is offered to graduate students at UT Dallas. Our ultimate goal would be for all graduate students to have health insurance, and UT Dallas health insurance would be included as part of a graduate student's acceptance package.

The Graduate Student Assembly is a registered student organization at The University of Texas at Dallas.
All graduate students of UTDallas are considered members and eligible to participate in the Graduate Student Assembly.
Membership shall be open to students of UTDallas regardless of sex, race, color, religion, age, national origin, disability, or veteran status. Membership must be limited to UTDallas students, faculty, and staff.
